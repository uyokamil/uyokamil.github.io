Little Sapling

Team Size 1
Project Duration 3 days
Target Platform PC
Engine / Language Unity / C#

"Little Sapling" is a tower defense game I developed in just 72 hours for "My First Game Jam: Winter 2023." The game, inspired by the theme of "Growth," challenges players to protect a sapling from waves of evil lumberjacks. Drawing on mechanics from childhood favorites like Bloons TD, "Little Sapling" offers two levels of difficulty—easy and medium—to cater to a range of players.

In "Little Sapling," players strategically place buildings that automatically attack incoming enemies. Key defenses include the Spitting Poisonous Frog, which attacks with poison; the Beehive, which releases bees to sting lumberjacks; and the Mushroom Stump, which slows down enemies. Players control an orbit camera around a small, rectangular world to manage these defenses. Despite the intense time constraint, I focused on creating a functional and engaging gameplay loop. Essential game mechanics were prioritized, with unfortunately little playtesting. This resulted in there being some bugs, but the overall game is solid. This project highlighted my ability to deliver a complete, enjoyable game under tight deadlines.

The game can be downloaded and played from itch.io!
